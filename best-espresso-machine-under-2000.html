How to choose

Under $2000, the real decision is about the kind of experience you want.

Some buyers want a machine that feels more involved and rewarding to use. Others want something smoother, faster, and easier to fit into the morning.

The best machine in this range is not simply the one with the longest feature list. It is the one that suits your routine, your taste, and the way you want the kitchen to feel every day.

Our take

Buy Breville for balance, Fellow for design, and De’Longhi for approachability.

Breville remains the safest recommendation for most buyers in this range because it combines a premium feel with a very livable daily workflow.

Fellow is the stronger aesthetic choice, while De’Longhi works well for buyers who want premium espresso without a steeper learning curve.
