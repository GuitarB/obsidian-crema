Breville vs Jura espresso machines — which is better?

Breville and Jura are two of the most popular premium espresso brands, but they are built for different types of buyers. One focuses on control and hands-on brewing, while the other focuses on convenience and full automation.

Main difference

Manual vs automatic experience.

Breville machines give you more involvement in the process. You grind, tamp, and dial in your espresso, which many coffee enthusiasts prefer.

Jura machines are designed to do everything automatically. They are ideal for buyers who want excellent coffee with minimal effort and a cleaner countertop experience.
