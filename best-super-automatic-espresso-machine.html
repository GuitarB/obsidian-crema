What makes it super automatic

These machines are built to remove the ritual, not just simplify it.

A super automatic machine handles more of the process for you, from grinding and brewing to milk-based drinks on many models. The appeal is consistency and speed.

For buyers who want great coffee without turning espresso into a hobby, this is usually the most satisfying category to buy from.

Our take

Buy DeLonghi for value and livability. Buy Jura for a more elevated luxury feel.

DeLonghi makes the stronger case for most buyers because the machines are easier to justify and easier to live with financially while still delivering the core benefit of super automatic ownership.

Jura is the better fit when the budget is higher and the goal is a more polished, more premium experience on the counter every day.
